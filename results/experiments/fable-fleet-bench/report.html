E1 — plan terseness does instruction detail pay for itself?

Same 10 tasks × 3 seeds on C18 qwen3-30B. Three supervisor plan styles: a one-line terse ask, a medium plan (spec + “no fences, no explanation”), and a detailed plan with examples and a constraint list.

E3 — fix informativeness is detailed feedback worth its tokens?

C18 thinking-off (abundant clean logic failures), medium plans, 6 seeds. Two scripted fixes: informative (quotes the failing case) vs minimal (“wrong, try again”).

A kick is as good as a coach. At this difficulty tier the worker repairs itself on retry; quoting the failure adds cost, not repair. Cheapest supervision: just “try again.”
